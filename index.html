Target performance
Beneficial targets are treated as lower bounds for strength, energy dissipation, hinge relocation and lower-bound drift capacity. Column-face stress and PEEQ targets are treated as upper bounds. When J1, J2 or J3 is active, the ranked list is screened by that criterion whenever at least one FE-backed case in the selected beam context satisfies it.
